1. T-SHIRT

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Translate the sentences into equations:
    • 1 Mango + 1 Pear = $3.30 (Equation 1)
    • 2 Mangoes + 5 Pears = $9.00 (Equation 2)
  2. Multiply Equation 1 by 2 to make the number of mangoes match Equation 2: $2 \times (\text{1M + 1P} = 3.30) \rightarrow \text{2M + 2P} = 6.60$ (Equation 3).
  3. Subtract Equation 3 from Equation 2 to find the difference: $(2\text{M} + 5\text{P}) - (2\text{M} + 2\text{P}) = 9.00 - 6.60$.
  4. This simplifies to $3\text{P} = 2.40$.
  5. Solve for the price of one pear: $\text{P} = 2.40 \div 3 = 0.80$. One pear costs $0.80.
  6. Substitute the price of a pear back into Equation 1: $\text{M} + 0.80 = 3.30$.
  7. Solve for the price of one mango: $\text{M} = 3.30 - 0.80 = 2.50$.
Answer: One mango costs $2.50.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Ubah kalimat menjadi persamaan:
    • 1 Mangga + 1 Pir = $3,30 (Persamaan 1)
    • 2 Mangga + 5 Pir = $9,00 (Persamaan 2)
  2. Kalikan Persamaan 1 dengan 2 agar jumlah mangga sama dengan Persamaan 2: $2 \times (\text{1M + 1P} = 3,30) \rightarrow \text{2M + 2P} = 6,60$ (Persamaan 3).
  3. Kurangkan Persamaan 3 dari Persamaan 2 untuk mencari selisihnya: $(2\text{M} + 5\text{P}) - (2\text{M} + 2\text{P}) = 9,00 - 6,60$.
  4. Persamaan ini menjadi $3\text{P} = 2,40$.
  5. Cari harga satu pir: $\text{P} = 2,40 \div 3 = 0,80$. Harga satu pir adalah $0,80.
  6. Substitusikan harga satu pir kembali ke Persamaan 1: $\text{M} + 0,80 = 3,30$.
  7. Cari harga satu mangga: $\text{M} = 3,30 - 0,80 = 2,50$.
Jawaban: Harga satu mangga adalah $2,50.
2. VENDING MACHINE
Singapore Math Approach: Worst-Case Scenario (Pigeonhole Principle)
To be absolutely sure of an outcome, we must consider the worst possible luck. This means we will get one of every different item before we get a duplicate.

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Identify the different categories of items. There are 3 colors: Yellow, Green, and Blue.
  2. Imagine the worst-case scenario: you pick one of each color before getting a pair.
    • 1st dollar: You get a Yellow ball. (You don't have a pair yet).
    • 2nd dollar: You get a Green ball. (You don't have a pair yet).
    • 3rd dollar: You get a Blue ball. (You still don't have a pair).
  3. At this point, you have spent $3 and have one ball of each color.
  4. The very next ball you buy (the 4th ball) *must* match one of the colors you already have. This guarantees a pair.
  5. Therefore, you need to buy 4 balls to be certain. Since each ball costs $1, the total cost is $4.
Answer: You need to spend at least $4.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Identifikasi kategori benda yang berbeda. Ada 3 warna: Kuning, Hijau, dan Biru.
  2. Bayangkan skenario terburuk: Anda mengambil satu dari setiap warna sebelum mendapatkan pasangan.
    • Dolar ke-1: Anda mendapat bola Kuning. (Belum punya pasangan).
    • Dolar ke-2: Anda mendapat bola Hijau. (Belum punya pasangan).
    • Dolar ke-3: Anda mendapat bola Biru. (Masih belum punya pasangan).
  3. Pada titik ini, Anda telah menghabiskan $3 dan memiliki satu bola dari setiap warna.
  4. Bola berikutnya yang Anda beli (bola ke-4) *pasti* akan cocok dengan salah satu warna yang sudah Anda miliki. Ini menjamin Anda mendapatkan sepasang.
  5. Oleh karena itu, Anda perlu membeli 4 bola untuk benar-benar yakin. Karena setiap bola berharga $1, total biayanya adalah $4.
Jawaban: Anda perlu mengeluarkan uang minimal $4.
3. STAMPS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Let's represent Sam's total stamps as 1 (a whole).
  2. He gave $\dfrac{1}{7}$ to Ann. The fraction remaining is $1 - \dfrac{1}{7} = \dfrac{6}{7}$.
  3. He gave $\dfrac{1}{3}$ of the *remainder* to Mary. The amount Mary received is $\dfrac{1}{3} \times \dfrac{6}{7} = \dfrac{6}{21} = \dfrac{2}{7}$ of the total stamps.
  4. Susan received what was left after Ann and Mary took their shares.
  5. Total given away = (Ann's share) + (Mary's share) = $\dfrac{1}{7} + \dfrac{2}{7} = \dfrac{3}{7}$.
  6. Susan's share = $1 - (\text{Total given away}) = 1 - \dfrac{3}{7} = \dfrac{4}{7}$.
Answer: Susan received $\dfrac{4}{7}$ of Sam's stamps.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Anggap total prangko Sam sebagai 1 (satu kesatuan utuh).
  2. Dia memberikan $\dfrac{1}{7}$ kepada Ann. Sisa pecahannya adalah $1 - \dfrac{1}{7} = \dfrac{6}{7}$.
  3. Dia memberikan $\dfrac{1}{3}$ dari *sisanya* kepada Mary. Jumlah yang diterima Mary adalah $\dfrac{1}{3} \times \dfrac{6}{7} = \dfrac{6}{21} = \dfrac{2}{7}$ dari total prangko.
  4. Susan menerima sisa setelah Ann dan Mary mengambil bagian mereka.
  5. Total yang diberikan = (Bagian Ann) + (Bagian Mary) = $\dfrac{1}{7} + \dfrac{2}{7} = \dfrac{3}{7}$.
  6. Bagian Susan = $1 - (\text{Total yang diberikan}) = 1 - \dfrac{3}{7} = \dfrac{4}{7}$.
Jawaban: Susan menerima $\dfrac{4}{7}$ dari prangko Sam.

4. SAW

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. The key is to understand that to get 'n' pieces, you need to make 'n - 1' cuts.
  2. To get 6 pieces, the carpenter makes $6 - 1 = 5$ cuts.
  3. The time taken for 5 cuts is 60 minutes.
  4. Therefore, the time for one cut is $60 \text{ minutes} \div 5 \text{ cuts} = 12$ minutes per cut.
  5. To get 11 pieces, the carpenter needs to make $11 - 1 = 10$ cuts.
  6. The total time for 10 cuts will be $10 \text{ cuts} \times 12 \text{ minutes/cut} = 120$ minutes.
Answer: It will take him 120 minutes.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Kuncinya adalah memahami bahwa untuk mendapatkan 'n' bagian, perlu dilakukan 'n - 1' kali pemotongan.
  2. Untuk mendapatkan 6 bagian, tukang kayu melakukan $6 - 1 = 5$ kali pemotongan.
  3. Waktu yang dibutuhkan untuk 5 kali pemotongan adalah 60 menit.
  4. Maka, waktu untuk satu kali potong adalah $60 \text{ menit} \div 5 \text{ potongan} = 12$ menit per potong.
  5. Untuk mendapatkan 11 bagian, tukang kayu perlu melakukan $11 - 1 = 10$ kali pemotongan.
  6. Total waktu untuk 10 kali potong adalah $10 \text{ potongan} \times 12 \text{ menit/potong} = 120$ menit.
Jawaban: Dia akan membutuhkan waktu 120 menit.
5. GIFT
Singapore Math Approach: Shortage & Surplus
This method focuses on the difference between the two scenarios to find the number of units (students).

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. The difference in payment per student between the two scenarios is $\$3 - \$2 = \$1$.
  2. The total difference in the outcome is from a shortage of \$4 to a surplus of \$3. The total swing is $\$4 + \$3 = \$7$.
  3. This \$7 difference was created because each student paid an extra \$1.
  4. $\text{Number of students} = \frac{\text{Total Difference}}{\text{Difference per student}} = \frac{\$7}{\$1} = 7 \text{ students}.$
  5. To find the cost of the gift, use one of the scenarios:
    • Scenario 1: $(7 \text{ students} \times \$2) + \$4 \text{ shortage} = \$14 + \$4 = \$18$.
    • Scenario 2: $(7 \text{ students} \times \$3) - \$3 \text{ surplus} = \$21 - \$3 = \$18$.
Answer: The gift costs $18.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Selisih iuran per murid antara kedua skenario adalah $\$3 - \$2 = \$1$.
  2. Total selisih hasil akhirnya adalah dari kekurangan \$4 menjadi kelebihan \$3. Total perubahannya adalah $\$4 + \$3 = \$7$.
  3. Selisih \$7 ini muncul karena setiap murid membayar tambahan \$1.
  4. $\text{Jumlah murid} = \frac{\text{Total Selisih}}{\text{Selisih per murid}} = \frac{\$7}{\$1} = 7 \text{ murid.}$
  5. Untuk mencari harga hadiah, gunakan salah satu skenario:
    • Skenario 1: $(7 \text{ murid} \times \$2) + \text{kekurangan } \$4 = \$14 + \$4 = \$18$.
    • Skenario 2: $(7 \text{ murid} \times \$3) - \text{kelebihan } \$3 = \$21 - \$3 = \$18$.
Jawaban: Harga hadiahnya adalah \$18.
6. CLOCK

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Calculate the total real time that has passed from 8:00 A.M. Sunday to 8:00 A.M. Monday. This is exactly 24 hours.
  2. Calculate the total time the clock has lost. It loses 5 minutes every hour.
  3. Total time lost = $24 \text{ hours} \times 5 \text{ minutes/hour} = 120$ minutes.
  4. Convert the lost minutes to hours: $120 \text{ minutes} = 2$ hours.
  5. The correct time is 8:00 A.M. on Monday. The faulty clock is 2 hours behind.
  6. Time shown on the clock = 8:00 A.M. - 2 hours = 6:00 A.M.
Answer: The clock will show 6:00 A.M.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Hitung total waktu nyata yang telah berlalu dari pukul 8:00 pagi hari Minggu hingga 8:00 pagi hari Senin. Ini adalah tepat 24 jam.
  2. Hitung total waktu yang hilang dari jam tersebut. Jam itu kehilangan 5 menit setiap jam.
  3. Total waktu yang hilang = $24 \text{ jam} \times 5 \text{ menit/jam} = 120$ menit.
  4. Ubah menit yang hilang menjadi jam: $120 \text{ menit} = 2$ jam.
  5. Waktu yang benar adalah pukul 8:00 pagi hari Senin. Jam yang rusak terlambat 2 jam.
  6. Waktu yang ditunjukkan jam = 8:00 pagi - 2 jam = 6:00 pagi.
Jawaban: Jam tersebut akan menunjukkan pukul 6:00 pagi.

7. CALCULATORS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Set up the equations:
    • $4C + 3P = 65.20$ (Eq 1)
    • $2C + 5P = 41.00$ (Eq 2)
  2. Double Eq 2 to match the number of calculators in Eq 1: $2 \times (2C + 5P = 41.00) \rightarrow 4C + 10P = 82.00$ (Eq 3).

8. TARTS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. The number of tarts leaves a remainder of 1 when divided by 3 or 4. This means the number is 1 more than a common multiple of 3 and 4.
  2. The Least Common Multiple (LCM) of 3 and 4 is 12.
  3. So, the number of tarts must be in the form of $(12 \times k) + 1$. Let's list possible numbers: 13, 25, 37, 49, ...
  4. The number of tarts must also be a multiple of 5 (since there is no remainder when giving 5 to each child).
  5. From our list, we check which number is a multiple of 5.
  6. 13 is not a multiple of 5.
  7. 25 is a multiple of 5.
  8. The smallest such number is 25.
Answer: The smallest number of tarts is 25.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Jumlah kue tart jika dibagi 3 atau 4 akan bersisa 1. Ini berarti jumlahnya adalah 1 lebih dari kelipatan persekutuan 3 dan 4.
  2. Kelipatan Persekutuan Terkecil (KPK) dari 3 dan 4 adalah 12.
  3. Jadi, jumlah kue tart harus dalam bentuk $(12 \times k) + 1$. Mari kita daftar bilangan yang mungkin: 13, 25, 37, 49, ...
  4. Jumlah kue tart juga harus merupakan kelipatan 5 (karena tidak ada sisa saat memberikan 5 kepada setiap anak).
  5. Dari daftar kita, kita periksa bilangan mana yang merupakan kelipatan 5.
  6. 13 bukan kelipatan 5.
  7. 25 adalah kelipatan 5.
  8. Bilangan terkecil yang memenuhi adalah 25.
Jawaban: Jumlah kue tart terkecil adalah 25.
9. AGE

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Sally's current age is 12. Her cousin's age is found by switching the digits, which is 21.
  2. The age difference between them is $21 - 12 = 9$ years. This difference will always be the same.
  3. We need to find the next pair of two-digit ages that are reversals of each other and have a difference of 9. Let's check:
    • 12 & 21 (Difference: 9) - Current ages.
    • 23 & 32 (Difference: 9) - This is the next pair.
  4. This will happen again when Sally is 23 years old.
  5. To find out how many years from now that will be, subtract her current age: $23 - 12 = 11$ years.

10. BARRETTES
Singapore Math Approach: Constant Difference (After Change)
We analyze the difference between the two people. When one gives to the other, the difference changes by twice the amount given.

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Before: The difference between Rita and Zoe is 24. (Rita = Zoe + 24)
  2. Change: Rita gives 5 to Zoe. Rita loses 5, Zoe gains 5. The gap between them shrinks by $5 + 5 = 10$.
  3. After: The new difference is $24 - 10 = 14$.
  4. We are also told that after the exchange, Rita has twice as many as Zoe (Ratio R:Z = 2:1).
  5. In this 2:1 ratio, the difference is $2 \text{ units} - 1 \text{ unit} = 1$ unit.
  6. This 1 unit of difference is equal to the new difference of 14. So, 1 unit = 14.
  7. The question asks for the number of barrettes Rita had left (her "After" amount). Rita's "After" amount is 2 units.
  8. Rita's final amount = $2 \times 14 = 28$.
Answer: Rita had 28 barrettes left.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Sebelum: Selisih antara Rita dan Zoe adalah 24. (Rita = Zoe + 24)
  2. Perubahan: Rita memberikan 5 kepada Zoe. Rita kehilangan 5, Zoe mendapat 5. Jarak di antara mereka menyusut sebesar $5 + 5 = 10$.
  3. Sesudah: Selisih yang baru adalah $24 - 10 = 14$.
  4. Kita juga diberi tahu bahwa setelah pertukaran, Rita memiliki dua kali lebih banyak dari Zoe (Rasio R:Z = 2:1).
  5. Dalam rasio 2:1 ini, selisihnya adalah $2 \text{ unit} - 1 \text{ unit} = 1$ unit.
  6. 1 unit selisih ini sama dengan selisih baru yaitu 14. Jadi, 1 unit = 14.
  7. Pertanyaan menanyakan jumlah jepit rambut yang tersisa milik Rita (jumlah "Sesudah"). Jumlah "Sesudah" Rita adalah 2 unit.
  8. Jumlah akhir Rita = $2 \times 14 = 28$.
Jawaban: Rita memiliki sisa 28 jepit rambut.

13. WEALTH

  3. Pecahan yang tersisa untuk amal adalah sisa pertama dikurangi bagian anak-anak: $\dfrac{7}{10} - \dfrac{3}{10} = \dfrac{4}{10}$.
  4. Sederhanakan pecahan untuk amal: $\dfrac{4}{10} = \dfrac{2}{5}$.
Jawaban: Dia menyumbangkan $\dfrac{2}{5}$ dari kekayaannya untuk amal.
14. COINS
Singapore Math Approach: Working Backwards
Since we know the final state (total and ratio), we can calculate the "After" amounts and then reverse the transaction.

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. After: The total is 144 coins, and the ratio of Juan to Melissa is 2:1.
  2. This means there are $2 + 1 = 3$ units in total.
  3. Value of 1 unit = $144 \div 3 = 48$ coins.
  4. After the exchange, Melissa had 1 unit = 48 coins. Juan had 2 units = $2 \times 48 = 96$ coins.
  5. Reverse the change: The change was "Juan gave 16 coins to Melissa". To reverse it, we give 16 back to Juan and take 16 from Melissa.
  6. Juan at first = $96 (\text{after}) + 16 = 112$ coins.
  7. Melissa at first = $48 (\text{after}) - 16 = 32$ coins.
Answer: Juan had 112 coins at first.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Sesudah: Totalnya 144 koin, dan rasio Juan terhadap Melissa adalah 2:1.
  2. Ini berarti ada total $2 + 1 = 3$ unit.
  3. Nilai 1 unit = $144 \div 3 = 48$ koin.
  4. Setelah pertukaran, Melissa memiliki 1 unit = 48 koin. Juan memiliki 2 unit = $2 \times 48 = 96$ koin.
  5. Balikkan perubahan: Perubahannya adalah "Juan memberi 16 koin kepada Melissa". Untuk membalikkannya, kita kembalikan 16 ke Juan dan ambil 16 dari Melissa.
  6. Juan pada awalnya = $96 (\text{sesudah}) + 16 = 112$ koin.
  7. Melissa pada awalnya = $48 (\text{sesudah}) - 16 = 32$ koin.
Jawaban: Juan memiliki 112 koin pada awalnya.

Step-by-step Solution (English):

    • Total time: 14 min 26 s = $(14 \times 60) + 26 = 866$ seconds.

  6. Selesaikan untuk G: $G = 108 \div 12 = 9$. Ada 9 ekor kambing.
  7. Jumlah sapi adalah $C = 2G = 2 \times 9 = 18$.
Jawaban: Ada 18 ekor sapi.
30. BOOKS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. We are given $\dfrac{2}{3}$ of Eddie's books (E) = $\dfrac{3}{7}$ of Linda's books (L). $\dfrac{2}{3}E = \dfrac{3}{7}L$.
  2. To make the numerators the same, find the LCM of 2 and 3, which is 6.
    • $\dfrac{2 \times 3}{3 \times 3}E = \dfrac{6}{9}E$
    • $\dfrac{3 \times 2}{7 \times 2}L = \dfrac{6}{14}L$
  3. So, $\dfrac{6}{9}$ of Eddie's books = $\dfrac{6}{14}$ of Linda's books. This tells us the ratio of their books is E:L = 9:14. Eddie has 9 units and Linda has 14 units.
  4. Linda has 15 more books than Eddie. The difference in units is $14 - 9 = 5$ units.
  5. So, 5 units = 15 books, which means 1 unit = 3 books.
  6. Eddie's books = $9 \text{ units} \times 3 = 27$ books.
  7. Linda's books = $14 \text{ units} \times 3 = 42$ books.
  8. Total books = $27 + 42 = 69$.
Answer: They have 69 books in total.
