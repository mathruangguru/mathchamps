1. SUE'S AGE

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Find total ratio parts: The ratio is 2:1, so the total parts are $2 + 1 = 3$.
  2. Find the value of 1 part: The combined age is 63. So, 1 part = $63 \div 3 = 21$ years.
  3. Calculate current ages:
    • Sue's age (1 part) = $1 \times 21 = 21$ years.
    • Mother's age (2 parts) = $2 \times 21 = 42$ years.
  4. Find the time difference: For Sue to reach her mother's age, $42 - 21 = 21$ years must pass.
  5. Calculate future ages: In 21 years, Sue will be $21+21=42$ and her mother will be $42+21=63$.
  6. Find their combined future age: $42 + 63 = 105$ years.
Answer: 105 years.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Cari total bagian rasio: Perbandingannya 2:1, jadi total bagiannya adalah $2 + 1 = 3$.
  2. Cari nilai 1 bagian: Jumlah umur adalah 63. Jadi, 1 bagian = $63 \div 3 = 21$ tahun.
  3. Hitung umur sekarang:
    • Umur Sue (1 bagian) = $1 \times 21 = 21$ tahun.
    • Umur Ibu (2 bagian) = $2 \times 21 = 42$ tahun.
  4. Cari selisih waktu: Agar umur Sue sama dengan umur Ibu, perlu waktu $42 - 21 = 21$ tahun.
  5. Hitung umur di masa depan: Dalam 21 tahun, Sue akan berumur $21+21=42$ dan Ibu akan berumur $42+21=63$.
  6. Cari jumlah umur mereka di masa depan: $42 + 63 = 105$ tahun.
Jawaban: 105 tahun.
2. FRUITS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Fruits sold: Started with 400, left with 120. Total sold = $400 - 120 = 280$.
  2. Set up equations: Let G = grapefruits, M = mangoes.
    • $G + M = 400$
    • $\dfrac{3}{4}G + \dfrac{2}{3}M = 280$
  3. Solve the equations: From the first equation, $G = 400 - M$. Substitute into the second: $$ \begin{aligned} \frac{3}{4}(400 - M) + \frac{2}{3}M &= 280 \\ 9(400 - M) + 8M &= 3,360 \quad (\text{multiply by 12}) \\ 3,600 - 9M + 8M &= 3,360 \\ 3,600 - M &= 3,360 \\ M &= 240 \end{aligned} $$
  4. Find the fraction: The initial fraction of mangoes was $\dfrac{240}{400} = \dfrac{3}{5}$.
Answer: The fraction of mangoes was $\dfrac{3}{5}$.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  3. Selesaikan persamaan: Dari persamaan pertama, $G = 400 - M$. Substitusikan: $$ \begin{aligned} \frac{3}{4}(400 - M) + \frac{2}{3}M &= 280 \\ 9(400 - M) + 8M &= 3.360 \quad (\text{kali dengan 12}) \\ 3.600 - 9M + 8M &= 3.360 \\ 3.600 - M &= 3.360 \\ M &= 240 \end{aligned} $$

3. MARBLES

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Fraction of blue/yellow: Red is $\dfrac{3}{10}$, Green is $\dfrac{2}{5} = \dfrac{4}{10}$. Together they are $\dfrac{7}{10}$. So, Blue + Yellow = $1 - \dfrac{7}{10} = \dfrac{3}{10}$ of total.
  2. Individual fractions: The ratio of Blue:Yellow is 2:1 (3 parts).
    • Blue = $\dfrac{2}{3}$ of $\dfrac{3}{10} = \dfrac{2}{10}$ of total.
    • Yellow = $\dfrac{1}{3}$ of $\dfrac{3}{10} = \dfrac{1}{10}$ of total.
  3. Set up equation: Number of blue marbles is 17 less than red. Let T be total marbles. $$ \begin{aligned} (\text{Red marbles}) - (\text{Blue marbles}) &= 17 \\ \frac{3}{10}T - \frac{2}{10}T &= 17 \\ \frac{1}{10}T &= 17 \end{aligned} $$
  4. Solve for T: If $\dfrac{1}{10}$ of the total is 17, then the total is $17 \times 10 = 170$.
Answer: 170 marbles.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Pecahan biru/kuning: Merah $\dfrac{3}{10}$, Hijau $\dfrac{2}{5} = \dfrac{4}{10}$. Totalnya $\dfrac{7}{10}$. Jadi, Biru + Kuning = $1 - \dfrac{7}{10} = \dfrac{3}{10}$ dari total.
  2. Pecahan individu: Rasio Biru:Kuning adalah 2:1 (3 bagian).
    • Biru = $\dfrac{2}{3}$ dari $\dfrac{3}{10} = \dfrac{2}{10}$ dari total.
    • Kuning = $\dfrac{1}{3}$ dari $\dfrac{3}{10} = \dfrac{1}{10}$ dari total.
  3. Buat persamaan: Kelereng biru 17 lebih sedikit dari merah. Misal T adalah total kelereng. $$ \begin{aligned} (\text{Kelereng merah}) - (\text{Kelereng biru}) &= 17 \\ \frac{3}{10}T - \frac{2}{10}T &= 17 \\ \frac{1}{10}T &= 17 \end{aligned} $$
  4. Cari nilai T: Jika $\dfrac{1}{10}$ dari total adalah 17, maka totalnya adalah $17 \times 10 = 170$.
Jawaban: 170 kelereng.

4. CARS S & T

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Time analysis: They travel for 8 hours before meeting (7am to 3pm). After meeting, Car S travels 5 more hours to finish. Total journey for Car S = $8+5=13$ hours.
  2. Distance relationship: The distance Car S covers in 5 hours (after meeting) is the same distance Car T covered in 8 hours (before meeting).
  3. Ratio of distances covered before meeting: This means the ratio of distances they covered to reach the meeting point is $d_S:d_T = 8:5$. The total distance is $8+5 = 13$ parts.
  4. Analyze Car T's remaining journey: After meeting, Car T had to cover the distance Car S covered, which is 8 parts. Car T covers 5 parts of distance in 8 hours. So, its rate is $\frac{5}{8}$ parts/hour.
  5. Calculate distance and solve: In the next 5 hours, Car T covers $5 \times \frac{5}{8} = \frac{25}{8}$ parts. $$ \begin{aligned} (\text{Remaining distance}) - (\text{Distance covered}) &= 150 \text{ km} \\ 8 \text{ parts} - \frac{25}{8} \text{ parts} &= 150 \text{ km} \\ \frac{39}{8} \text{ parts} &= 150 \text{ km} \\ 1 \text{ part} &= \frac{150 \times 8}{39} = \frac{400}{13} \text{ km} \end{aligned} $$
  6. Total Distance: The total distance is 13 parts. Total = $13 \times \dfrac{400}{13} = 400$ km.
Answer: 400 km.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Analisis waktu: Mereka berjalan 8 jam sebelum bertemu (7 pagi - 3 sore). Setelah bertemu, Mobil S butuh 5 jam lagi. Total perjalanan Mobil S = $8+5=13$ jam.
  2. Hubungan jarak: Jarak yang ditempuh Mobil S dalam 5 jam (setelah bertemu) sama dengan jarak yang ditempuh Mobil T dalam 8 jam (sebelum bertemu).
  3. Rasio jarak sebelum bertemu: Artinya perbandingan jarak tempuh mereka sampai titik pertemuan adalah $J_S:J_T = 8:5$. Jarak total adalah $8+5 = 13$ bagian.
  4. Analisis sisa perjalanan Mobil T: Setelah bertemu, Mobil T harus menempuh jarak yang sudah ditempuh Mobil S, yaitu 8 bagian. Mobil T menempuh 5 bagian jarak dalam 8 jam. Jadi, kecepatannya $\frac{5}{8}$ bagian/jam.
  5. Hitung jarak dan selesaikan: Dalam 5 jam berikutnya, Mobil T menempuh $5 \times \frac{5}{8} = \frac{25}{8}$ bagian. $$ \begin{aligned} (\text{Sisa jarak}) - (\text{Jarak ditempuh}) &= 150 \text{ km} \\ 8 \text{ bagian} - \frac{25}{8} \text{ bagian} &= 150 \text{ km} \\ \frac{39}{8} \text{ bagian} &= 150 \text{ km} \\ 1 \text{ bagian} &= \frac{150 \times 8}{39} = \frac{400}{13} \text{ km} \end{aligned} $$
  6. Jarak Total: Jarak total adalah 13 bagian. Total = $13 \times \dfrac{400}{13} = 400$ km.
Jawaban: 400 km.

6. PAGE NUMBERS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Conditions: The page number must (1) contain the digit '5', and (2) be divisible by 5.
  2. Divisible by 5: This means the number must end in a 0 or a 5.
  3. Case 1: Numbers ending in 5. All these numbers automatically contain the digit '5' and are divisible by 5. We count them: 5, 15, 25, ..., 495. $$ \text{Count} = \frac{495-5}{10} + 1 = 49 + 1 = 50 \text{ numbers} $$
  4. Case 2: Numbers ending in 0. We only need to count those that also contain a '5' elsewhere. These are: 50, 150, 250, 350, 450, and 500. This is a total of 6 numbers.
  5. Total Count: The two cases have no overlap. Total = (Case 1) + (Case 2) = $50 + 6 = 56$.
Answer: 56.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Kondisi: Nomor halaman harus (1) mengandung angka '5', dan (2) habis dibagi 5.
  2. Habis dibagi 5: Artinya angka tersebut harus berakhiran 0 atau 5.
  3. Kasus 1: Angka berakhiran 5. Semua angka ini otomatis mengandung digit '5' dan habis dibagi 5. Kita hitung: 5, 15, 25, ..., 495. $$ \text{Jumlah} = \frac{495-5}{10} + 1 = 49 + 1 = 50 \text{ angka} $$
  4. Kasus 2: Angka berakhiran 0. Kita hanya perlu menghitung yang juga mengandung angka '5' di tempat lain. Angka-angka tersebut adalah: 50, 150, 250, 350, 450, dan 500. Ini totalnya 6 angka.
  5. Jumlah Total: Kedua kasus tidak tumpang tindih. Total = (Kasus 1) + (Kasus 2) = $50 + 6 = 56$.

8. RED & GREEN BALLS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Set up the system of equations: Let R = red balls, G = green balls.
    • Eq 1: $\dfrac{1}{4}R + \dfrac{2}{5}G = 21$
    • Eq 2: $\dfrac{3}{4}R + \dfrac{4}{5}G = 47$
  2. Notice a relationship: Eq 2 has terms that are related to Eq 1. Let's multiply Eq 1 by 2: $$ 2 \times \left(\frac{1}{4}R + \frac{2}{5}G\right) = 2 \times 21 \implies \frac{2}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G = 42 $$
  3. Subtract the new equation from Eq 2: $$ \begin{aligned} \left(\frac{3}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G\right) - \left(\frac{2}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G\right) &= 47 - 42 \\ \frac{1}{4}R &= 5 \\ R &= 20 \end{aligned} $$
  4. Substitute R back into Eq 1 to find G: $$ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{4}(20) + \frac{2}{5}G &= 21 \\ 5 + \frac{2}{5}G &= 21 \\ \frac{2}{5}G &= 16 \\ G &= 16 \times \frac{5}{2} = 40 \end{aligned} $$
Answer: 20 red balls and 40 green balls.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  1. Buat sistem persamaan: Misal R = bola merah, G = bola hijau.
    • Pers 1: $\dfrac{1}{4}R + \dfrac{2}{5}G = 21$
    • Pers 2: $\dfrac{3}{4}R + \dfrac{4}{5}G = 47$
  2. Perhatikan hubungannya: Suku di Pers 2 berhubungan dengan Pers 1. Mari kalikan Pers 1 dengan 2: $$ 2 \times \left(\frac{1}{4}R + \frac{2}{5}G\right) = 2 \times 21 \implies \frac{2}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G = 42 $$
  3. Kurangkan persamaan baru dari Pers 2: $$ \begin{aligned} \left(\frac{3}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G\right) - \left(\frac{2}{4}R + \frac{4}{5}G\right) &= 47 - 42 \\ \frac{1}{4}R &= 5 \\ R &= 20 \end{aligned} $$
  4. Substitusikan R ke Pers 1 untuk mencari G: $$ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{4}(20) + \frac{2}{5}G &= 21 \\ 5 + \frac{2}{5}G &= 21 \\ \frac{2}{5}G &= 16 \\ G &= 16 \times \frac{5}{2} = 40 \end{aligned} $$

  3. Calculate time taken: Time is the reciprocal of the rate. $$ \text{Time} = \frac{1}{\text{Combined Rate}} = \frac{1}{5/24} = \frac{24}{5} = 4.8 \text{ minutes} $$

13. RECTANGULAR CARDBOARD

Step-by-step Solution (English):

    • Orientation 1: Align 8cm side with 50cm side, 6cm with 27cm side. $\lfloor\frac{50}{8}\rfloor \times \lfloor\frac{27}{6}\rfloor = 6 \times 4 = 24$ rectangles.
    • Orientation 2: Align 6cm side with 50cm side, 8cm with 27cm side. $\lfloor\frac{50}{6}\rfloor \times \lfloor\frac{27}{8}\rfloor = 8 \times 3 = 24$ rectangles.
Answer: 24 rectangles.

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Bahasa Indonesia):

  2. Potongan Sederhana (Tanpa Rotasi):
    • Orientasi 1: Sisi 8cm sejajar sisi 50cm, sisi 6cm sejajar sisi 27cm. $\lfloor\frac{50}{8}\rfloor \times \lfloor\frac{27}{6}\rfloor = 6 \times 4 = 24$ buah.
    • Orientasi 2: Sisi 6cm sejajar sisi 50cm, sisi 8cm sejajar sisi 27cm. $\lfloor\frac{50}{6}\rfloor \times \lfloor\frac{27}{8}\rfloor = 8 \times 3 = 24$ buah.

15. TRIANGLES

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Similarity Principle: All equilateral triangles are similar to each other.
  2. Ratio of Areas: For any two similar figures, the ratio of their areas is equal to the square of the ratio of their corresponding linear measurements (like sides or heights).

  4. Selesaikan sistem persamaan: Substitusikan S ke persamaan jumlah. $$ \begin{aligned} (\frac{3}{5}J + 52) + J &= 260 \\ \frac{8}{5}J &= 208 \\ J &= 208 \times \frac{5}{8} = 130 \end{aligned} $$
  5. Cari jumlah awal Shirley: $S = 260 - J = 260 - 130 = 130$.
Jawaban: Shirley memiliki 130 koin pada awalnya.
30. TV SETS

Step-by-step Solution (English):

  1. Express Q and R in terms of y:
    • Shop P = 210.
    • Shop Q = P + y = $210 + y$.
    • Shop R = $3 \times Q = 3(210+y) = 630 + 3y$.
  2. Part 1: How many more does R have than P? $$ R - P = (630 + 3y) - 210 = 420 + 3y $$
  3. Part 2: If y = 110, find the total.
    • P = 210.
    • Q = $210 + 110 = 320$.
    • R = $3 \times 320 = 960$.
    • Total = $210 + 320 + 960 = 1,490$.
Answer: R has (420+3y) more than P. Total is 1,490.
