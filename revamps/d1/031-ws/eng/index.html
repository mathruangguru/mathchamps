SUBTRACTION PROBLEM SET

Instruction: Read the questions carefully, look at the bar models, then calculate the answers.
1. Adi has 5 balls. Budi has 2 fewer balls than Adi. How many balls does Budi have?
Answer: balls.
2. Siti has 7 pencils. Dayu has 3 fewer pencils than Siti. How many pencils does Dayu have?
Answer: 7 - 3 = pencils.
3. Mother bought 8 oranges. Father bought 4 fewer oranges than Mother. How many oranges did Father buy?
Answer: oranges.
