Subtraction with Regrouping

If the ones of the number being subtracted are not enough, move 1 rod (10 ones) from the tens place to become 10 units in the ones place.

→EXCHANGE
1 Ten = 10 Ones
Section A: Picture Concepts
1. Find the value of 32 - 5:
Since 2 is not enough to subtract 5, 1 rod (10 units) from the tens is moved to become 10 units in the ones.
32
→
Remaining: 2 Tens + 7 Ones
2. Find the value of 24 - 8:
Since 4 is not enough to subtract 8, 1 rod (10 units) from the tens is moved to become 10 units in the ones.
24
→
Remaining: 1 Ten + 6 Ones
