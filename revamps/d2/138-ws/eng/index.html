Let's Learn! (Division Concepts)

Division can be done by grouping objects.
Count the total number of objects, then divide them into equal groups.
Example: 6 divided by 2 (Group every 2 objects)
Result:
There are 3 groups formed.
So, 6 : 2 = 3.
1. There are 6 circles. Circle every 2 circles to make one group.
How many groups are formed? groups.
2. There are 8 boxes. Circle every 4 boxes to make one group.
How many groups are formed? groups.
3. Divide 9 triangles into groups of 3 triangles.
How many groups of triangles are there? groups.
4. Mom has 10 marbles. Each bag contains 5 marbles.
How many bags does Mom need?
