Let's Learn! (Division Concept)

Division is grouping objects into equal parts or sharing equally.

Part A: Multiple Choice

1. There are 6 apples. Mother wants to share them into 2 plates equally.
How many apples are on each plate?
2. There are 8 cookies. Budi groups them into 2 cookies per group.
How many groups are formed?
3. Siti has 10 star stickers. She sticks 5 stars on each book.
How many books have stickers?
4. There are 12 marbles. Shared equally among 3 children.
Each child gets ... marbles.
