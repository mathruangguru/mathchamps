Practice 1: Sharing Objects (Visual)

Instruction: Share the objects below into the available boxes or groups equally.
1. There are 6 balls. Share them equally into 2 boxes. How many balls are in each box?
2. Mother has 8 cookies. She shares them into 4 plates equally. How many cookies are on each plate?
Answer: cookies.
3. Share these 10 stars between 2 children equally.
Each child gets stars.
