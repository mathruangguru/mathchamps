Code : D3-S170

ODD AND EVEN

Concept: Pairing Numbers
EVEN Numbers

Can be paired, no leftovers.

Example: 4 is Even.

Ones Digit:
0, 2, 4, 6, 8

ODD Numbers

There is one leftover when paired.

Example: 5 is Odd.

Ones Digit:
1, 3, 5, 7, 9
