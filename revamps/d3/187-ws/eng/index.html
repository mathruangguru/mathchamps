WORKSHEET: SUBTRACTION WITH REGROUPING

Concept Example: 52 - 18 (Regrouping Tens)
Problem: We have 2 ones, want to subtract 8 ones.
Not enough!
Solution: Regroup 1 Ten.
  • 5 Tens become 4 Tens.
  • 1 Ten is exchanged for 10 Ones.
  • Now there are 12 Ones (2 + 10).
  • 12 - 8 = 4.
