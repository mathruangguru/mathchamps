ONE-STEP SUBTRACTION

Bar Model Concept (Part-Whole)
In subtraction, we have a Whole (Total). If one Part is taken away or lost, we look for the other Remaining Part.
Total: 500
Lost: 200
Left: ?
A. Choose the Most Appropriate Answer
1. Budi has 542 marbles. He gave 125 marbles to his friend. How many marbles does Budi have left now?
2. A bakery made 800 breads. By noon, 347 breads were sold. Use the bar model below to find the unsold bread!
Total: 800
Sold: 347
Left: ?
Answer: breads.
3. The school library has 925 storybooks. 188 books are being borrowed by students. How many books are still on the shelf?
