FINDING THE STARTING NUMBER

Concept: Working Backwards (Finding the Starting Value)

Section A: Multiple Choice & Fill in the Blanks (Use Bar Models)
1. Mother made some sponge cakes. Father ate 4 cakes. Then, Mother gave 6 cakes to the neighbor. Now there are 10 cakes left on the plate. How many cakes did Mother make at first?
Number Sentence: + + =
Answer: cakes.
2. A bus departed carrying passengers. At the first stop, 5 people got off. At the second stop, 8 people got off. Now there are 20 people left on the bus. How many passengers were on the bus at first?
3. Dani has some savings. He bought ice cream for Rp 2,000 and bought a toy for Rp 15,000. Dani's money left is Rp 5,000. How much money did Dani have at first?
Answer: Rp
4. A flock of birds perched on a tree. 10 birds flew north. Then, 6 birds flew south. There are still 4 birds left on the tree. How many birds were on the tree at first?
5. A balloon seller has a stock of balloons. 5 balloons popped. Then, 12 balloons were sold. In the afternoon, there were 30 balloons left.
Answer: balloons.
