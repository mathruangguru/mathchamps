Problem: Mother bought 50 eggs. Then, 10 eggs broke on the way. Then, Mother cooked 15 eggs for dinner. How many eggs are left now?
Method: Subtract the amounts taken away from the total.
(50 - 10 - 15 = 25) or (50 - 25 = 25). Left = 25 eggs.
