Discovery 4
Advanced Addition & Subtraction
Two-Step Addition Involving Three People
Code : D4-S313
Singapore Math Method: Bar Models
Example: Adi has 10 marbles. Budi has 5 more marbles than Adi. Cici has 3 fewer marbles than Budi. How many marbles does Cici have?
STEP 1 (Find Budi)
10 + 5 = 15
STEP 2 (Find Cici)
15 - 3 = 12
Practice Questions (Part 1)
1. Ali collected 25 shells. Beni collected 12 more shells than Ali. Ciko collected 8 fewer shells than Beni. How many shells did Ciko collect?
Answer: shells
2. In the library, Shelf A contains 100 books. Shelf B contains 25 fewer books than Shelf A. Shelf C contains 15 more books than Shelf B. How many books are on Shelf C?
Answer: books
3. A farmer harvested apples into three baskets. Basket I contains 50 apples. Basket II contains 10 more apples than Basket I. Basket III contains 20 more apples than Basket II. How many apples are in Basket III?

Practice Questions (Part 2)
4. Ani's height is 120 cm. Budi is 5 cm taller than Ani. Citra's height is the same as Ani's height minus 2 cm. What is the difference between Budi's and Citra's height?
Answer: cm
5. Mother bought three types of fruits. There are 15 Oranges. There are 5 more Apples than Oranges. There are 8 fewer Mangoes than Apples. How many Mangoes did Mother buy?
Answer: mangoes
6. In a video game: Danu's score is 200 points. Edo's score is 50 points lower than Danu's. Feri's score is 25 points higher than Edo's. What is Feri's score?
7. Grandfather planted trees for three weeks. The first week he planted 30 trees. The second week he planted 10 more trees than the first week. The third week he planted the same amount as the combined amount of the first and second week. How many trees were planted in the third week?
Answer: trees
8. Rina's pocket money is Rp 5,000. Sinta's pocket money is Rp 2,000 more than Rina's. Tono's pocket money is Rp 1,500 less than Sinta's. How much is Tono's pocket money?
Answer: Rp
