Two-Step Word Problems on Cutting Objects
Code : D4-S322
Instruction: Use bar models to help you solve the problems. Remember, these are two-step word problems!
1. Mother has a rope that is 100 cm long. She cuts 30 cm to tie newspapers, and cuts another 45 cm for a clothesline. How many cm of rope does Mother have left now?
2. Father has a pipe that is 90 cm long. He cuts it into 3 parts. The first part is 20 cm long. The second part is 35 cm long. What is the length of the third part (leftover pipe)?
Answer: cm
3. Siti cuts her decorative ribbon. She gives 25 cm to Lani and 15 cm to Dayu. Siti still has 40 cm of ribbon left. What was the length of Siti's ribbon at first?
4. Mr. Budi cuts a long piece of wood into 2 pieces of equal length. If the length of one piece is 45 cm, and there is still a small leftover piece of wood 10 cm long that is unused. What was the length of Mr. Budi's wood at first?
Answer: cm
5. A wire is 200 cm long. It is cut 50 cm for the bedroom light. Then, it is cut again for the garden light. If the remaining wire is now 70 cm, what is the length of the wire used for the garden light?
Hint: Find the total known wire first (Bedroom light + Left).

6. Edo has a scout stick. He cuts the top end 10 cm because it is broken. He cuts the bottom end 10 cm to make it even. The length of Edo's stick is now 140 cm. What was the length of Edo's stick before it was cut?
Answer: cm
7. Rani has a blue ribbon that is 150 cm long. She cuts the ribbon into 3 parts: Part A, Part B, and Part C. The length of Part A is 40 cm. The length of Part B is 50 cm. What is the length of Part C?
8. Thinking Challenge (Discovery)
There are two ribbons, Red Ribbon and Yellow Ribbon. The Red Ribbon is 100 cm long. The Yellow Ribbon is 80 cm long. Both ribbons are cut 20 cm each. What is the difference in length between the two ribbons after being cut?
Answer: cm
9. A wire is cut into 4 parts of equal length. The length of each piece is 5 cm. There is still a remaining wire of 2 cm that is not cut. What was the total length of the wire at first?
10. Budi cuts a rope that is 1 meter long. (Remember: 1 meter = 100 cm). He cuts it into two parts. The first piece is 10 cm longer than the second piece. If the second piece is 45 cm long, what is the length of the remaining rope?
Watch out: Calculate the length of the first piece first, then find the total length of both pieces, then find the remainder from 100 cm. If there is no remainder, write 0.
Answer: cm
