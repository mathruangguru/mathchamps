Part B: Advanced Logic & Word Problems
5. A bamboo pole is 12 meters long. Father wants to cut it into small pieces that are 3 meters long each.
How many times must Father cut the bamboo?
Answer: times.
6. Compare the following two ropes before they are cut:
  • Rope A: Cut 1 time. Each piece is 5 m.
  • Rope B: Cut 3 times. Each piece is 2 m.
Which rope is longer before being cut?
7. Andi has a wire. He cuts the wire into 6 parts. Each part is 10 cm long.
If Andi wants to cut the same wire into just 2 parts, what is the length of one part?
Answer: cm.
8. A long sponge cake is cut 4 times. If the length of each piece of cake is 3 cm, what is the length of the whole sponge cake?
Answer: cm.
