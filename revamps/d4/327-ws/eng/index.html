Concept Example: Bar Model (Mass & Difference)
Question: The mass of Box A is 200 grams. The total mass of Box A and Box B is 500 grams. What is the difference in mass between Box B and Box A?
Step 1: Find Mass of B
B = 500 - 200 = 300g
Step 2: Find the Difference
300 - 200 = 100g
