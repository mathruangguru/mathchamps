Concept Example: Comparison Model
Andi's mass is 20 kg. Budi is 5 kg heavier than Andi. Cici is 2 kg lighter than Budi. What is Cici's mass?
Step 1: Budi = 20 + 5 = 25 kg.
Step 2: Cici = 25 - 2 = 23 kg.
