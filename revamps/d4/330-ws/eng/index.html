Discovery 4
Advanced Measurement
Finding Start Time
Code : D4-S330
Instruction: Use the Timeline to count backward from the end time to the start time.
1. Concept Visualization:
The painting class ends at 10.00 a.m. The class lasts for 1 hour. Before painting, students do preparation for 1 hour. What time do they start preparation?
2. Andi finished playing football at 16.00.
He played football for 1 hour. Before playing, he warmed up for 30 minutes. What time did Andi start warming up?
3. Mother finished baking a cake at 11.30 a.m.
The cake was baked for 30 minutes. Mother prepared the batter for 30 minutes before baking. What time did Mother start making the cake?
4. A cartoon movie ended at 09.00 a.m.
The movie consisted of two parts. The second part lasted 30 minutes. The first part lasted 30 minutes. What time did the movie start?
Answer: Time

5. Budi arrived home at 14.00 (2 p.m.).
The journey home took 1 hour. Before going home, he stopped at a bookstore for 1 hour. What time did Budi leave school (before going to the bookstore)?
6. Siti finished doing homework at 08.00 p.m.
Math homework was done in 30 minutes. Indonesian homework in 30 minutes. What time did Siti start studying?
Answer: Time
7. Challenge High Ability
The birthday party ended at 05.15 p.m. The cake cutting ceremony lasted 15 minutes. Before that, there were games for 1 hour. What time did the games start?
8. Father finished washing the car and watering the plants at 10.00 a.m.
Washing the car took 30 minutes. Watering the plants took 15 minutes. Father's total working time was 45 minutes. What time did Father start?
Answer: Time
