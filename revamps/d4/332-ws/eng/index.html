Finding the Quantity Per Group
Code : D4-S332
Study Guide: Bar Model

To find the quantity in each group, we divide the Total by the number of boxes (groups).

Example: Total 10 divided by 2 boxes = 5 items per box.

Section A: Multiple Choice
Choose the most appropriate answer to fill in the question mark (?).
1. There are 15 apples distributed equally into 3 baskets. How many apples are in each basket?
2. Mom has 20 cakes. She puts them into 4 plates equally.
3. Mr. Budi has 18 pencils. He ties them into 2 equal bundles.
