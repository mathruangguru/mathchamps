Section A: Multiple Choice & Fill in the Blanks (Visualization)

1. Budi's age is 8 years. His sister, Sari, is 3 years older than Budi. What is the sum of their ages?

3. Bayu's age is 9 years. Bayu is 2 years older than Cika. What is the sum of their ages?
Logic: If Bayu is older, it means Cika is younger (the bar is shorter).
