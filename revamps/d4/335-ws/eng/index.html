Code : D4-S335
Example Problem
Problem: Budi bought 3 boxes of donuts. Each box contains 4 donuts. Then, Budi ate 2 donuts. How many donuts does Budi have left now?
Step 1: Find the Total (Multiplication)
3 x 4 = 12
Step 2: Remainder (Subtraction)
Total Donuts: 12
Eaten: 2
12 - 2 = 10
Answer: 10 Donuts
