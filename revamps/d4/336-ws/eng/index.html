Study Guide: Two-Step Models

Section A: Multiple Choice & Fill in the Blanks
1. Example: Mom has 12 sponge cakes. She buys 8 more sponge cakes. She puts all the cakes onto plates. Each plate holds 5 cakes. How many plates are needed?
2. Budi has 24 marbles. He gives 6 marbles to his younger brother. The remaining marbles are put into bags. Each bag contains 6 marbles. How many bags does Budi have?
Answer: bags

3. There are 15 roses in the garden. Dad plants 5 more flowers. All the flowers are tied into bouquets. Each bouquet contains 5 roses. How many bouquets are there now?
4. There are 30 red apples in the shop. The seller adds 6 green apples. The apples are put into baskets. One basket holds 9 apples. How many baskets are full?
Answer: baskets
5. Siti has 20 pencils. She gives 5 pencils to her friend. The remaining pencils are put into new pencil cases that hold 5 pencils each. How many new pencil cases are needed?
6. The Teacher brings 40 notebooks. It turns out 4 books are damaged and must be thrown away. The good books are distributed to students in groups. One group gets 9 books. How many groups receive books?
Answer: groups
7. There are 16 goldfish in the pond. Uncle adds 4 more goldfish. The fish will be moved to small aquariums. Each aquarium only holds 5 fish. How many aquariums are needed?
