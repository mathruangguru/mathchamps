Section B. Solve the following word problems. Use scratch paper to draw the models.
4.
Dad weighed his suitcase and it was 14 kg. Dad's suitcase is 2 times as heavy as the younger sibling's backpack. What is the weight of the younger sibling's backpack?
Answer: kg.
5.
In a video game adventure, the price of one encyclopedia is 24 gold coins. That price is 4 times as much as the price of one comic book. How many gold coins are needed to buy the comic book?
Answer: gold coins.
6.
While practicing running, the distance of the outer track is 35 meters. That distance is 5 times as long as the distance of the track used for warming up. What is the distance of the warm-up track?
Answer: meters.
7.
The 'Lezat' bakery sold 27 chocolate donuts today. The chocolate donuts sold were 3 times as many as the cheese donuts. How many cheese donuts were sold?
Answer: donuts.
8.
The fuel tank of a large truck contains 40 liters of diesel. The content of the tank is 8 times as much as the content of a small jerrycan. How many liters of diesel can the small jerrycan hold?
Answer: liters.
9.
A train trip out of town takes 21 hours. That time is 3 times as long as a trip using a short-route bus. How many hours are needed if using the short-route bus?
Answer: hours.
10.
In a storybook, a python is described as being 32 meters long. The length of that snake is 4 times as long as a common snake. What is the actual length of the common snake?
Answer: meters.
