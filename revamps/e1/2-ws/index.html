Let's Discover! (Ayo Temukan!)

When we read 4-digit numbers, we read the thousands first, then the hundreds, and finally the last two digits.
(Saat membaca bilangan 4 angka, baca ribuannya dulu, lalu ratusan, dan terakhir dua angka belakang.)

4,321
→
"Four thousand, three hundred and twenty-one"
"Empat ribu, tiga ratus dua puluh satu"
