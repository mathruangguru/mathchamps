3. Compare the numbers 6,780 and 6,790. Bandingkan bilangan 6.780 dan 6.790.
  • The digit in the thousands place is the same (6).
  • The digit in the hundreds place is the same (7).
Look at the tens place. (Lihat tempat puluhan.)
8 tens is than 9 tens.
(Write smaller or greater / Tulis lebih kecil atau lebih besar)
So, 6,780 is than 6,790.
