Problem: Mom bought 125 cm of ribbon. She cut 5 cm for gift decoration. The rest was cut into 6 equal parts for handicrafts. What is the length of each handicraft piece?

Initial Total = 125 cm

Remainder: 5
6 Equal Parts (Units)
Step 1: Find the remainder to be divided.
Initial Total - Unused Remainder
125 - 5 = 120
Step 2: Divide by the number of units.
Remaining amount √∑ Number of Parts
120 √∑ 6 = 20
Answer: Each piece is 20 cm long.
