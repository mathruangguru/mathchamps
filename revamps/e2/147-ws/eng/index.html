FINDING THE REMAINING AMOUNT

Calculating the Remainder After Repeated Use

Worked Example

Problem: Uncle has a stock of 100 kg of rice. He puts the rice into 8 sacks, where each sack contains 10 kg. How much rice is left unpacked?

Initial Total = 100 kg
Used (8 sacks × 10 kg)
?
Left
?
Step 1: Calculate the total used.
Number of Groups × Items per Group
8 × 10 = 80 kg
Step 2: Calculate the remainder.
Initial Total - Total Used
100 - 80 = 20 kg
Answer: The remaining rice is 20 kg.
