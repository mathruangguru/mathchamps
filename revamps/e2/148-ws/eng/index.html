FINDING THE NUMBER OF GROUPS

How many items can be bought/made?

Worked Example

Problem: Uncle harvests 50 kg of corn. He keeps 2 kg to cook for himself. The rest is put into sacks that hold 6 kg each. How many sacks does Uncle need?

Initial Total = 50 kg
Put into Sacks (6 kg each)
...
? Sacks
Kept
2 kg
Step 1: Calculate the amount to be divided.
Initial Total - Remainder
50 - 2 = 48 kg
Step 2: Divide by the content per sack.
Total Divided ÷ Content per Sack
48 ÷ 6 = 8 Sacks
Answer: Uncle needs 8 sacks.
