Problem: The weight of 1 Large Box is equal to the weight of 3 Small Boxes. If the weight of 2 Small Boxes is 10 kg, what is the weight of 1 Large Box?

Step 1: Find value of the small unit.
Weight of 2 Small Boxes = 10 kg
1 Small Box = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 kg
Step 2: Use the relationship.
1 Large = 3 × Small
1 Large = 3 × 5 = 15 kg
Answer: The weight of 1 Large Box is 15 kg.
