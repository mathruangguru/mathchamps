How Many Items?

Let's recall!

Bob has Rp20,000. He bought a bread for Rp8,000. The remaining money will be used to buy several candies costing Rp3,000 each. How many candies did Bob get?

At first = Rp20,000
Bread
...
Rp8,000
Left = Rp12,000
Money Left:
Rp20,000 − Rp8,000 = Rp12,000
Number of Candies:
Rp12,000 ÷ Rp3,000 = 4 pieces
So, Bob got 4 candies.
