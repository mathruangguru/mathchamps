What is the Difference, What is the Price?

Let's observe the example!

Saka and Danis have a total money of Rp62.800. Saka has Rp18.800 more than Danis. How much money does each of them have?

Danis
1 unit
Saka
Rp18.800 (Difference)
Total: Rp62.800
Find the value of 2 units:
Rp62.800 − Rp18.800 = Rp44.000
Find Danis's money (1 unit):
1 unit → Rp44.000 ÷ 2 = Rp22.000
Find Saka's money:
Rp22.000 + Rp18.800 = Rp40.800
So, Danis has Rp22.000 and Saka has Rp40.800.
