What Is the Item's Price?

Let's look at an example!

Ariel brought Rp20,000. He bought a book for Rp12,000 and a pencil. Ariel's remaining money is now Rp3,000. What is the price of the pencil?

At first = Rp20,000
Book
Pencil (?)
Left
Rp12,000
?
Rp3,000
Total Spent:
Rp20,000 − Rp3,000 = Rp17,000
Price of Pencil:
Rp17,000 − Rp12,000 = Rp5,000
So, the price of Ariel's pencil is Rp5,000.
