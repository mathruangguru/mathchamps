Advanced Skill – Money
Finding the value of one item from the total and relationship of three items
Kode : E2-S160

Analyzing Three Objects

Let's look at an example!

Nico, Leo, and Kenzo have a total of Rp20,000. Nico has Rp5,000. Leo has Rp3,000 more than Nico. How much money does Kenzo have?

Nico
Rp5,000
Leo
+ Rp3,000
Kenzo
?
Total:
Rp20,000
Leo's Money:
Rp5,000 + Rp3,000 = Rp8,000
Kenzo's Money:
Rp20,000 − (Rp5,000 + Rp8,000) = Rp7,000
So, Kenzo's money is Rp7,000.
