1.

Kenzo has a total of Rp10,000. He has 1 note of Rp2,000 and several notes of Rp1,000. How many Rp1,000 notes does Kenzo have?

Kenzo
Total = Rp10.000
Rp2k
...
Denomination Rp1,000
Remaining money → Rp10.000 − Rp2.000 = Rp
Number of notes → Rp ÷ Rp1.000 = notes.
