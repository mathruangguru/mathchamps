What Is The Total Price?

Guided example:

The price of a Notebook is Rp15,000. The price of a School Bag is Rp10,000 more expensive than the notebook. What is the total price of the two items?

Notebook
Rp15,000
School Bag
Rp10,000
?
  1. Find Bag Price:
    Rp15,000 + Rp10,000 = Rp25,000
  2. Find Total Price:
    Rp15,000 + Rp25,000 = Rp40,000
So, the total price of the two items is Rp40,000.
