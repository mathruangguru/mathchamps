How Much is One Item?

Let's Look at an Example!

The price of a pencil case is the same as the price of 3 notebooks. Andra bought 1 pencil case and 2 notebooks for a total price of Rp25.000. What is the price of 1 notebook?

Total: Rp25.000 (5 Units)
Unit Logic
1 pencil case = 3 notebook units.
Total spending = 3 units + 2 units = 5 units.
Notebook Price
5 units → Rp25.000
1 unit → Rp25.000 ÷ 5 = Rp5.000
So, the price of 1 notebook is Rp5.000.
