What is the Price of the Other Item?

Let's look at an example!

Ardi bought 2 school bags and 3 notebooks for a total of Rp190.000. If the price of 1 school bag is Rp50.000, what is the price of 1 notebook?

Total: Rp190.000

1. Find the total price of 2 bags:
2 × Rp50.000 = Rp100.000
2. Find the total price of 3 notebooks:
Rp190.000 − Rp100.000 = Rp90.000
3. Find the price of 1 notebook:
Rp90.000 ÷ 3 = Rp30.000
So, the price of 1 notebook is Rp30.000.
