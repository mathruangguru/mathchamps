Finding the quantity of items if the total price and unit price are known
Code : E2-S177

How Many Items?

Let's look at an example!

Mother bought several boxes of delicious donuts for family snacks. The price of one box is Rp15.000. If Mother spent a total of Rp75.000, calculate how many boxes of donuts Mother brought home!

Total Cost: Rp75.000
...
Rp15.000
There are 5 Boxes
Steps:
Quantity of Items = Total Money ÷ Unit Price
Calculation:
Number of Boxes = Rp75.000 ÷ Rp15.000 = 5 boxes
So, the number of donut boxes Mother bought is 5 boxes.
