How Many to Sell?

Let's look at an example!

Cindy bought several candies for Rp3.000 each. She then sold them for Rp3.500 each. How many candies must Cindy sell to make a profit of Rp15.000?

Step 1:
Find the profit for each candy:
Rp3.500 − Rp3.000 = Rp500
Step 2:
Find the number of candies to sell:
Rp15.000 ÷ Rp500 = 30 candies
So, Cindy must sell 30 candies.
