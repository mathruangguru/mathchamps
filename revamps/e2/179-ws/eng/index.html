Finding item price from comparing two shopping groups
Code : E2-S179

Equating Units

Let's look at an example!

The price of 1 book and 1 pencil is Rp22.000. The price of 2 books and 7 pencils is Rp79.000. What is the price of 1 book?

Equate (Multiply Group 1 by 2):
2 × Rp22.000 = Rp44.000
Find price difference (5 pencils):
Rp79.000 − Rp44.000 = Rp35.000
5 pencils cost Rp35.000.
Find price of 1 pencil:
Rp35.000 ÷ 5 = Rp7.000
1 pencil costs Rp7.000
Find price of 1 book:
1 book = Rp22.000 − Rp7.000 = Rp15.000
The price of one book is Rp15.000.
So, the price of 1 book is Rp15.000.
