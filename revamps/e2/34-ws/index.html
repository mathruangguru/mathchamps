Worked Example: The Bar Model Strategy

Problem: Mrs. Tan had 50 cookies. She kept 2 cookies for herself and put the rest equally into 6 jars. How many cookies were in each jar?

Ibu Tan memiliki 50 kue. Dia menyimpan 2 kue dan memasukkan sisanya sama rata ke dalam 6 toples. Berapa kue di setiap toples?
Total = 50

Left: 2
6 Equal Jars (Units)
Step 1: Find the total used.
(Cari total yang digunakan)
Total − Leftover = Used

50 − 2 = 48 cookies.
Step 2: Divide by jars (units).
(Bagi dengan jumlah toples)
48 ÷ 6 = 8 cookies per jar.
Answer: There were 8 cookies in each jar.
