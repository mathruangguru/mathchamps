Learning Corner: The "Square Corner" Test

Remember: An Obtuse Angle is greater than a Right Angle (Square Corner).
Ingat: Sudut Tumpul lebih besar dari Sudut Siku-siku (Pojok Persegi).

Acute Angle

Smaller than a square corner

Right Angle

Exactly a square corner

Obtuse Angle

Greater than a square corner
