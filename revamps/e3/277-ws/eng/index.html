Concept Guide: Fraction Subtraction

To subtract fractions from whole numbers, we can use the **Regrouping** strategy. Break 1 whole number into an equivalent fraction (e.g., 1 = 4/4).

$$ 2 - \frac{3}{4} = 1\frac{4}{4} - \frac{3}{4} = 1\frac{1}{4} $$
