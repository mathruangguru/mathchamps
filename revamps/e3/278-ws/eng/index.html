Visual Guide: Part-Whole Model

To find a fraction of a set of objects, we can use a Bar Model. We divide the total number of objects into equal groups.
Example: What is \( \frac{3}{4} \) of 12?
Steps:
  1. Divide 12 into 4 equal parts. (1 part = \( 12 \div 4 = 3 \))
  2. Take 3 of those parts. (\( 3 \times 3 = 9 \))
  3. So, \( \frac{3}{4} \) of 12 is 9.
