Decimal Subtraction

Subtracting decimals with one decimal place.

Subtraction means **taking away** or **removing** a part.
Look at the picture below: The crossed out (X) part is what is subtracted.
0.5 - 0.2 = 0.3
Initially there are **5** bars (0.5).
We cross out **2** bars (0.2).
The remainder is **3** bars (0.3).
