Borrowing from Neighbors

If the number is less, borrow from the larger place value.

Regrouping Tenths

Example: 0.42 - 0.18.
We have 2 small squares, want to take away 8. Not enough!

  • Break it into 10 Small Squares.
  • Now there are 12 small squares. Subtract 5, remainder is 7.
