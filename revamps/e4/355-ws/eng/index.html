WORKSHEET: IDENTIFYING SYMMETRY

Understand the Concept! (Singapore Math Approach)
A figure is called Symmetrical if it can be folded into two parts that match exactly. The fold line is called the Line of Symmetry. If it cannot, it is called Asymmetrical.
