WORKSHEET: FINDING THE MISSING SIDE

Key Concept: Visual Approach
To find the side length if the Perimeter (P) and one side are known, we use the Half Perimeter concept.
Example Problem
Step 1: Find Half Perimeter
20 ÷ 2 = 10 cm (Total L + W)
Step 2: 10 - 6 = 4 cm (Width)
Part A: Visual Practice
1. The perimeter of the rectangle on the side is 24 cm. Its length is 8 cm. What is its width?
Working: 24 ÷ 2 = 12
Remaining = 12 - 8 = ...
Answer: cm
2. The perimeter of a photo frame is 50 cm. Its width is 10 cm. What is its length?
Answer: cm
Part B: Multiple Choice & Fill in the Blanks
3. If the Half Perimeter (length + width) is 15 cm, and the width is 6 cm, what is the length?
