WORKSHEET: FINDING THE MISSING SIDE

Visual Approach

Basic Concept
Remember the Area of Rectangle formula: Area = Length × Width
If the Area is known, we can find the missing side by division:
? = Area ÷ Other Side
Width = Area ÷ Length Length = Area ÷ Width
Part A: Visual Understanding (Short Answer)
1. Determine the length of the side marked with a question mark (?).
Answer: cm
2. Determine the missing width.
Answer: m
