WORKSHEET: AREA OF COMPOSITE FIGURES

Decomposition Strategy
To calculate the area of a composite figure:
  1. Split the figure into smaller squares or rectangles.
  2. Calculate the area of each small part (Length × Width).
  3. Add all areas to get the total area.
