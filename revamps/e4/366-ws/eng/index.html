WORKSHEET: PATH AREA EXPLORATION

Key Concept: Path Area
  • Think of the path area as the **difference** (subtraction).
  • Formula: Path Area = Area of Large Rectangle - Area of Small Rectangle.
  • Observe dimensions carefully:
    Outer Length = Inner Length + (2 x Path Width)
Section A: Identifying Dimensions & Area
1. A rectangular garden measures 10 m x 6 m. A path of width 2 m is made around the garden.
What are the Total Length and Width (Large Rectangle)?
2. Using data from question 1, what is the area of the path (gray region)?
Step 1: Large Area = x = m²
Step 2: Small Area = 10 x 6 = 60 m²
Step 3: Path Area = Large Area - Small Area
Final Answer: m²
