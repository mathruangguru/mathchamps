WORKSHEET: COMPOSITE FIGURES

Visual Math Strategy
To calculate the Area of a composite figure, you can divide it into basic shapes (squares/rectangles). To calculate the Perimeter, imagine "pushing" the indented sides outward to form a complete rectangle.
Section A: Multiple Choice (Visual Analysis)
1. Look at the composite figure on the side. The figure is formed by combining two rectangles. What is the total area of the figure?
2. Using the same picture as in number 1. What is the perimeter of the composite figure?
Tip: Imagine the side lines pushed outward.
3. A large square paper has a side of 10 cm. A rectangle of size 6 cm x 4 cm is cut out in the center. Calculate the area of the shaded region.
Answer: cm²
