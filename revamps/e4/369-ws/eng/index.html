WORKSHEET: READING DATA TABLES

Key Concept: Reading Tables
  • Tables present data in rows and columns to make it easy to read.
  • Look at the Column Headers (top part) to know the type of data.
  • Compare numbers to find the greatest, smallest, or difference.
