WORKSHEET: VOLUME & ROUNDING

Worked Example

Problem: A large bottle contains 3 times as much water as a small cup. If the cup contains 250 ml, what is the total volume of both? Round to the nearest hundred.

Step 1: Find Volume of Bottle.
250 × 3 = 750 ml
Step 2: Find Total & Round.
250 + 750 = 1,000 ml
Round to nearest hundred: 1,000 ml
