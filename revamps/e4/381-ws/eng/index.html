WORKSHEET: Quantity Relationships

Worked Example

Problem: Budi has 3 times as much money as Ani. If the total amount of money they have is Rp 48,000, how much money does Ani have?

Step 1: Find the Total Units.
Budi's units = 3, Ani's units = 1.
Total Units = 3 + 1 = 4
Step 2: Find the value of 1 Unit (Ani).
48,000 ÷ 4 = 12,000
So, Ani has Rp 12,000.
