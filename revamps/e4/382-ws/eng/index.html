WORKSHEET: Comparison Models

Concept Guide (Worked Example)

Problem: A has 3 times as much money as B. The difference in their money is Rp 20,000. What is the total amount of money they have?

Step 1: Analyze the Model
Difference in units = 3 units (A) - 1 unit (B) = 2 units.
Step 2: Find the Value of 1 Unit
2 units = Rp 20,000
1 unit = Rp 20,000 ÷ 2 = Rp 10,000
Step 3: Calculate the Total
Total units = 3 units (A) + 1 unit (B) = 4 units.
Total = 4 × Rp 10,000 = Rp 40,000
