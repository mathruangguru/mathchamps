WORKSHEET: PROBLEM SOLVING

Worked Example

Problem: The sum of red and blue marbles is 80. There are 20 more red marbles than blue marbles. How many blue marbles are there?

Step 1: Subtract the Difference.
80 - 20 = 60

This is the sum of 2 equal parts.

Step 2: Divide by Two.
60 : 2 = 30
So, blue marbles = 30.
