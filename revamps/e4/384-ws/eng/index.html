WORKSHEET: DIFFERENCE & MULTIPLES

Worked Example

Problem: Number A is 3 times Number B. The difference between Number A and Number B is 10. What is the product of the two numbers?

Step 1: Find the Value of 1 Unit.
From the diagram, the difference is 2 units.
2 units = 10
1 unit = 10 ÷ 2 = 5
Step 2: Find the Product.
Number B (1 unit) = 5
Number A (3 units) = 3 × 5 = 15
Product = 15 × 5 = 75
Instruction: Use bar models to visualize "Difference" as a specific number of units.
