Solving Relationship Problems Menyelesaikan Masalah Hubungan (Model Batang)

Worked Example: Comparison Model

Problem: Seth has $50. Anna has $20 more than Seth. How much do they have altogether?

Seth punya $50. Anna punya $20 lebih banyak dari Seth. Berapa jumlah uang mereka berdua?
Step 1: Find Anna's amount.
(Cari jumlah milik Anna)
50 + 20 = 70
Step 2: Find Total.
(Cari Total)
50 + 70 = 120
