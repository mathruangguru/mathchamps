Volume & Rounding Volume dan Pembulatan (Model Batang)

Worked Example

Problem: A large bottle contains 3 times as much water as a small cup. If the cup holds 250 ml, how much do they hold altogether? Round to the nearest hundred.

Botol besar berisi 3 kali lebih banyak air daripada cangkir kecil. Jika cangkir berisi 250 ml, berapa total isi keduanya? Bulatkan ke ratusan terdekat.
Step 1: Find Bottle Volume.
(Cari Volume Botol)
250 × 3 = 750 ml
Step 2: Find Total & Round.
(Cari Total & Bulatkan)
250 + 750 = 1,000 ml
Round to nearest 100: 1,000 ml
