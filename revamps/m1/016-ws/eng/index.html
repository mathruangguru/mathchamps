Skill: Estimating differences using Rounding

STRATEGY EXAMPLE (VISUAL MATH)
Problem: Estimate the difference between 68 and 23 to the nearest ten.
STEP 1
(Round first)
68 → 70
23 → 20
➜
STEP 2
(Then subtract)
70 - 20 = 50
Number Line:
60 ------ 65 --- 68 - 70 (68 is close to 70)
20 -- 23 --- 25 ------ 30 (23 is close to 20)
