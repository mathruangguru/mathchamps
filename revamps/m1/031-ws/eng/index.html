WORKSHEET: DIVISION BY TENS

Thinking Strategy: Decomposition & Place Value
When dividing by 10, we shift each digit one place to the right. (Tip: Remove the last zero). For division by multiples of 10 (e.g., 20, 30), divide by 10 first, then divide the result by the leading digit.
Another Example (240 ÷ 20):
1. Divide by 10: 240 ÷ 10 = 24
2. Divide the rest: 24 ÷ 2 = 12
