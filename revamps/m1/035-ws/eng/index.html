Order of Operations Rules (Visual Guide)
In Singapore Math, we visualize the order of operations as steps on a ladder.
  1. Brackets: Work on what is inside this box first!
  2. Multiplication/Division: From left to right.
  3. Addition/Subtraction: From left to right.
Visual Example: 20 - (3 × 4)
