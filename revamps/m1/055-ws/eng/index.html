Solving Fraction Multiplication Word Problems
Code : M1-S055
Concept: The word "of" in fraction word problems indicates multiplication.
"half of 10" →
12
× 10 = 5
Part 1 - Use bar models to solve the problems.
1. Siti has 12 apples. She gives one-third of them to her sibling. How many apples does she give?
1 3
× 12 = apples
2. Mr. Budi has 15 kg of rice. He cooks two-thirds of it today. How many kg of rice does he cook?
2 3
× 15 = kg

Part 3 - Solve the following short word problems.
5.

Answer: students.
6.

Answer: eggs.
