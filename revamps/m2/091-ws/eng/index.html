WORKSHEET: MULTIPLYING DECIMALS

Concept: Place Value Shift
Remember the Singapore Math principle: "When multiplying a decimal number by 100, every digit moves two places to the left (becoming 100 times greater)."
Example: 0.35 × 100 = 35
