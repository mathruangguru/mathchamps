WORKSHEET: DECIMALS

Visual Concept: Shifting Place Values
In the Singapore Math approach, dividing by 10 means shifting every digit 1 place to the right on the place value table (or shifting the decimal point one step to the left).
Tens Ones . Tenths Hundredths
2 3 . 4
→ Shift 1 step to the RIGHT (Divide by 10) →
2 . 3 4
Example: 23.4 ÷ 10 = 2.34
Quick Tip: Move the decimal point 1 step to the left.
