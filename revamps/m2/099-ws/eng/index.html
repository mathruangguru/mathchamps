WORKSHEET: DIVIDING DECIMALS

Visual Concept: Place Value Chart
When we divide a decimal by 10, the value of each digit becomes smaller (1/10). Visually, we shift each digit 1 step to the RIGHT.
Tens Ones . Tenths Hundredths
3 . 2
→ Shift 1 step to the RIGHT (Divide by 10) →
0 0 . 3 2
Example: 3.2 ÷ 10 = 0.32
Quick Tip: The digits shift to the right, or the decimal point "seems" to shift to the left.
