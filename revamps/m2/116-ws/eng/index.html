CALCULATING DISCOUNT

Discount = Price Reduction. Price to Pay = Original Price - Discount.

Worked Example

Problem: A school bag costs Rp 200.000. It is discounted by 20%. What is the discount amount?

20% = 1/5 part. The total price is divided into 5 parts.
