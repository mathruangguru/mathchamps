CALCULATING TAX (GST)

Tax is an additional cost. Final Price = Original Price + Tax.

Worked Example

Problem: The price of a toy is Rp 100.000. Tax (GST) is charged at 10%. What is the total to be paid?

Original Price (100%) + Tax (10%) = Total (110%)

Price Rp 100.000
+ Tax
Total Pay Increases
Step 1: Calculate Tax
10% x 100.000
= 10/100 x 100.000
= Rp 10.000
Step 2: Final Price
Price + Tax
= 100.000 + 10.000
= Rp 110.000
