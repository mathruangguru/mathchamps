CALCULATING ANNUAL INTEREST

Interest = Extra Money from Savings or Loans.

Worked Example

Problem: Andi saves Rp 1,000,000 in a bank with 5% annual interest. How much interest does Andi receive after 1 year?

Interest = Interest % √ó Principal

Savings: Rp 1,000,000
5%
Total Money Increased
Formula
Interest = Interest % x Principal
Interest = 5% x 1,000,000
Calculation
= 5/100 x 1,000,000
= 5 x 10,000
= Rp 50,000
