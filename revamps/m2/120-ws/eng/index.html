FINDING THE TOTAL (SUM)

If we know the average, we can find the total sum.

Worked Example

Problem: Mother bought 3 boxes of pizza. On average, each box contains 4 slices of pizza. What is the TOTAL number of pizza slices Mother bought?

Average = 4 slices/box

3 Boxes
→

12 Slices
Quick Formula
Total = Average × Number of Items
Calculation
Average = 4, Number of Boxes = 3
4 × 3 = 12
