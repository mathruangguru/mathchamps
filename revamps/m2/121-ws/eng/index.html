FINDING THE NUMBER OF ITEMS

If we know the Total and the Average, how many items are there?

Worked Example

Problem: Mom has 12 pizza slices (Total). If the average box contains 4 slices, how many boxes does Mom have?

Combined Total (12)
12 Slices
→
Divided Equally (4 per box)
4
4
4
Result = 3 Boxes
Quick Formula
Number of Items = Total ÷ Average
Calculation
Total = 12, Average = 4
12 ÷ 4 = 3
