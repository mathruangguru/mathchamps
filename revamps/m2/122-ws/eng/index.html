AVERAGE (ADVANCED)

Using multi-step methods to find a new value.

Worked Example

Problem: The average test score of 3 students is 80. If Dedi's score is included, the average becomes 82 (now there are 4 students). What is Dedi's test score?

1. Initial Sum (3 Students)
Total = 3 × 80 = 240
2. New Sum (4 Students)
Total = 4 × 82 = 328
3. Difference (Dedi's Score)
328
-
240
=
88
Steps:
1 Find Initial Total: 3 × 80 = 240
2 Find New Total (after Dedi joins): 4 × 82 = 328
3 Dedi's Score = New Total - Initial Total = 328 - 240 = 88
Answer: Dedi's score is 88.
