Properties of Right-Angled Triangles

Worked Example

Concept: A right-angled triangle has one angle of 90° (marked with a square).
The sum of the three angles of a triangle is 180°. So, the sum of the other two angles is 90°.

→
Finding angle x:
Sum = 180°
Right angle = 90°
90° - 55° = 35°
x = 35°
Quick Tip: Angle x = 90° - Known angle
