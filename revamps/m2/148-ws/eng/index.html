WORKSHEET: WEIGHT DIFFERENCE LOGIC

Visual Approach: Finding the Container's Weight
If the total weight changes when the amount of contents changes, then the change in weight is caused by the weight of the contents added or removed. The weight of the container remains the same.
Section A: Short Answer (Find the weight of the container or contents)
1. A jar containing 6 marbles weighs 450 grams. The same jar containing 4 marbles weighs 350 grams.
a) What is the weight of 2 marbles? grams
b) What is the weight of 1 marble? grams
c) What is the weight of the empty jar? grams
2. The weight of a box with 10 books is 3,200 grams. If the box contains only 6 books, its weight becomes 2,000 grams.
What is the weight of the empty box?
Answer: grams
3. A bucket full of water weighs 8 kg. If the bucket is only half full of water, its weight is 4.5 kg.
What is the weight of the empty bucket? (Hint: The difference is the weight of half the water).
Answer: kg
