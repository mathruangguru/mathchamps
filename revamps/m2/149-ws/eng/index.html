WORKSHEET: AGE PROBLEMS

Visual Approach: Age Difference Remains Constant
The age difference between two people will never change, even as time passes. Use this constant difference to solve age ratio problems in the future.
Section A: Short Answer (Use age difference logic)
1. The age difference between Mother and Ani is 25 years. What will be their age difference in 5 years?
Answer: years
2. Budi's current age is 8 years and Father's is 38 years. In how many years will Father's age be 3 times Budi's age? (Hint: Their age difference is 30 years).
Answer: years
3. The age difference between the older sibling and younger sibling is 6 years. Three years ago, the older sibling's age was 2 times the younger sibling's age. What is the older sibling's current age?
Answer: years
