WORKSHEET: MONEY PROBLEMS

Visual Approach: Total & Difference
If the Total Price of several items and the Price Difference between items are known, we can make the units equal first by subtracting the Difference from the Total.
Section A: Short Answer (Solve the following problems)
1. Andi bought 1 Burger and 1 Drink for a total price of Rp45.000. The price of the Burger is Rp5.000 more expensive than the Drink.
a) If the price difference (Rp5.000) is subtracted from the total, what is the remainder?
b) What is the price of 1 Drink?
2. The sum of Budi's and Susi's savings is Rp150.000. Budi's savings is Rp10.000 less than Susi's.
How much is Budi's savings?
Answer: Rp
3. Pak Hasan bought 2 T-shirts and 1 pair of Pants for Rp200.000. The price of the Pants is the same as the price of a T-shirt.
(Hint: Because the prices are the same, assume there are 3 items with the same price).
What is the price of 1 T-shirt?
Answer: Rp
