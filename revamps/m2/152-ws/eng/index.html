WORKSHEET: MONEY TRANSFER

Visual Approach: Constant Total
When a money Transfer happens between two people, their individual amounts change, but their TOTAL money remains the SAME.
Section A: Short Answer (Focus on the Change in Amount)
1. Anton gives Rp5.000 to Budi.
a) Anton's money decreases by Rp
b) Budi's money increases by Rp
c) Does their total money change? (Yes/No)
2. Cici has Rp50.000 and Didi has Rp30.000. Cici gives Rp10.000 to Didi.
How much money does Cici have now? Rp
How much money does Didi have now? Rp
3. Eko gives some money to Feri. Now Eko's money decreases by Rp15.000.
By how many rupiahs does Feri's money increase?
Answer: Rp
