WORKSHEET: SPENDING & REMAINDER

Visual Approach: Part-Whole Model
Use a Bar Model to separate the Initial Money into Spending and Remainder parts. Pay attention to the multiple relationship between them.
Section A: Short Answer (Pay attention to the unit relationships)
1. Budi buys a toy for Rp15.000. His remaining money is 2 times the price of the toy.
a) How much is Budi's remaining money? (2 x 15.000) = Rp
b) How much money did Budi have at first? (15.000 + Remainder) = Rp
2. Clara spends Rp10.000 on snacks. The money left in her wallet is 4 times her spending.
Calculate Clara's total money at first!
Answer: Rp
3. Doni has some money. He buys a book for Rp25.000. Doni's remaining money is the same as the price of the book (1 time).
How much money did Doni have before buying the book?
Answer: Rp
