WORKSHEET: TOTAL UNIT PRICE

Visual Approach: Finding Value from the Total
If a multiple relationship is known (e.g., the price of 1 Book = 2 times the price of a Pencil) and the total combined price, we can find the unit price by adding up the units.
Example: The price of 1 Book = 2 times the price of a Pencil. The price of 1 Book + 1 Pencil = Rp15.000.
