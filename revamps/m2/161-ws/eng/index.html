WORKSHEET: DIVIDED REMAINDERS

Visual Approach: Combining Remainder Sub-groups
If a fraction of a group is known, and the remainder consists of several other items with known quantities, we can add the remainders to find the total value.
Example: $\frac{2}{5}$ of the marbles are Red. The remainder consists of 10 Blue and 20 Green marbles. What is the total number of marbles?
