High Ability - Fractions (Money)
Combination of Fractions & Amounts
Code : M2-S165

WORKSHEET: COUNT BACKWARD

Visual Approach: Combining the Remainder
If the money spent is given in fractions and Rupiah amounts, we can find the initial amount of money by looking at the remaining parts.
Example: Adi spent $\frac{1}{4}$ of his money. Then he gave Rp5,000 to his younger sibling. His remaining money is now Rp10,000.
